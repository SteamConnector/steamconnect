Welcome to SteamConnect.Site

This website is open source!

About

This website is designed for use with Discord, Steam and other apps that do not support the Steam Browser Protocol.
Use this website to automatically connect people to your server.
This website will NEVER record any of your data and only uses Cloudflare to manage the domain.

How to use this website:

https://steamconnect.site/?(IP or Domain):(Server port)/(Server password)

Examples:

  • https://steamconnect.site/?192.168.0.1:27015
  • https://steamconnect.site/?mycoolserver.example:27015
  • https://steamconnect.site/?192.168.0.1/password
